About

NNP was established by 3 founders: Cuong Phan, Thuy Nguyen, and Kim Nguyen in 2019. Fascinated by technological advances in 21st century, our founders saw an opportunity to revolutionize the oil industry by offering state-of-the-art solutions to fuel supply chain. Since establishing, NNP has served over 2,000 regular customers across the United States and deliver approximately 100,000 gallons on time with the best value & quality.
